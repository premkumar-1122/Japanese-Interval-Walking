JIW Tracker is an open-source fitness app that implements the Shinshu University-style interval walking protocol. Alternating between slow recovery walks and fast burn walks, this scientifically-backed method helps improve cardiovascular fitness and burn calories effectively.

<b>Key Features:</b>

• Interval Walking Workouts: Customizable slow/fast duration cycles with configurable total cycles per session
• Real-time Voice Coaching: Audio cues guide you through each interval phase transition
• Step Tracking: Uses the hardware step counter sensor for accurate, battery-efficient tracking
• Calorie Calculation: MET-based calorie burn estimation using your weight and walking speed
• Activity Recognition: Detects walking/running activity to ensure you're active during workouts
• Health Connect Sync: Sync completed sessions to Health Connect (steps, calories, distance, exercise data)
• Detailed Session History: View past workouts with cadence, pace, calories, and duration data
• Workout Dashboard: Start, pause, skip phases, or stop your interval sessions with an interactive notification
• Customizable Settings: Configure interval timing, weight units (kg/lb), and voice preferences
• Japanese Language Support: Full Japanese UI with translated voice cues for native speakers
• Onboarding Wizard: Guided setup to configure your preferences on first launch
• Light & Dark Theme: Automatic theme support based on system settings
• Data Export: Export your walking history for personal analysis

JIW Tracker respects your privacy. No account required, no data collection, and all data is stored locally on your device.

This app is fully open source under the GPL v3 license and contains no proprietary tracking or ads.
